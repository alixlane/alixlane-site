hi, i'm alix.

i like clear standards, good taste, and work that feels actually grounded.

i’ve spent the last few years around sales teams, operations, and customer-facing work — first in sports hospitality, then at pepsico. i’m interested in the part beneath the surface: how people work together, how expectations get set, and why some environments just run better than others.

about

i’m from new jersey, and i’m early in my career in a way that still feels open-ended in a good way. i know i like structure. i know i like thoughtful people. i know i care about details more than most.

this site is mostly just a small place to keep things simple: who i am, what i’ve done, and a few corners of the internet i actually use.

work

most recently i worked with pepsico in intern and associate roles. the work touched sales support, account upkeep, merchandising standards, and the day-to-day coordination that keeps things from drifting.

before that, i spent time in sports hospitality and operations, which taught me a lot about pacing, responsibility, and how much smoother things get when everyone is paying attention.

as a person

i’m a little particular in a way i’ve made peace with. i like spaces that feel considered. i like when people mean what they say. i like finding a cleaner way to do something, even if the original version technically worked.

outside of work i’m usually listening to music, reorganizing something, noticing typefaces, or overthinking why one room feels better than another. i’m not trying to be a brand here. just a person with a website.
